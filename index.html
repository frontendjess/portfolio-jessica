About

Jessica Mitchell
Designer + developer
American with a Swedish background
Based in Oslo, Norway

I fell in love with computers and programming from the age of 12, when I learned how to program a little turtle to make shapes, kind of creating geometrical artwork through the keyboard - who else remembers this turtle? This was probably the first time I felt what empowerement was.

From the little turtle, I moved onto webdesign, and fast-forward to present day, I am a front-end developer with a passion for creativity with an entreupreunal/marketing mindset - this is when you go to school for business but end up as a developer, hehe. - No worries, I am currently attending NOROFF, which soon in a year I will officially have my front end development degree.

Having an international background, attending US military school, then furthering my education at ECU in Australia for business. Life in my 20s lead me down a different path, which I enjoyed my young career. Moving to Norway, has in a way re-set my lifes path into where I have always wanted to be - webdesign & development.

On my free time, you can catch me, well, trying to keep up with my toddler, working on a couple side projects, and basking in any sunlight the Norwegian weather will allow us.
