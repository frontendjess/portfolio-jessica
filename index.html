About

Jessica Mitchell
Designer + developer
American with a Swedish background
Based in Oslo, Norway

My passion is creating modern and beautiful solutions that optimizes the user experience and establishing an impactful online presence.

Based in Oslo, I am a front-end developer with a strong-suit for creativity with an entreupreunal/marketing mindset - this is when you go to school for business but end up as a developer.

Having an international background, attending US military school, then furthering my education at ECU in Australia for business. Fast forward to present-day, as I am working on projects for webdesign and development, I am currently half-way through my front-end development 2 year degree at Noroff.

On my free time, you can catch me painting with my toddler, working on a couple side projects, reading autobiographies of inspirational women and basking in any sunlight the Norwegian weather will grant us.
